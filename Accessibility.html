Website Accessibility Standards

If our website is not compatible with your accessibility tools, please contact us at (602) 298-4200 or email info@azdem.org for support. Let us know what you what the obstacle is and we will look to remedy the situation in a timely manner. For users who have additional accessibility assistance need, we can be reached by Federal Information Relay Service at 1-800-877-8339.

Accessibility Web Design Guidelines


Our website was designed to comply with Section 508 as well as 2.0 A and AA which refer to website accessibility standards.
